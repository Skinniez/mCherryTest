Michelle Cherry, Broker Associate and Transaction Coordinator, has been licensed since 2000. Over the years, Michelle was a high producing agent with several companies, helped build a nationally recognized luxury real estate franchise in Austin, ventured on her own for several years before finally landing at Realty Austin in 2016. In 2006, Michelle obtained her Texas Real Estate Broker license, a license held by less than 1% of REALTORS in Texas. 

Additional designations include CRS (Certified Residential Specialist), ABR (Accredited Buyer’s Representative) and GRI (Graduate, REALTOR Institute). Michelle loves real estate and is looking forward to using her education and experience to make sure every agent has a smooth contract to close transaction. With Realty Austin’s unmatched and ever-evolving technology, Michelle is excited to begin this new venture. 

As a native Texan and Austinite for the past 20 years, Michelle and her family have grown to appreciate all the wonderful things Central Texas has to offer. Michelle is married to her husband Gary of 27+ years, has two daughters, two labradors, and two pugs. Michelle is an avid health nut and enjoys anything outdoors. She is committed to her community and continues to donate and volunteer monthly with her family.
